Don't jump into these waters!

Author:Paul Thompson
Date Posted:11/15/2020
School: Udacity Front End Development
Bio:
MLS (ASCP) - Graduated from UCF 2009
Microbiologist - Barnes Jewish Hospital 2010 - 2020

It's really cold...

Like seriously, look at all the snow everywhere! Can you see the boat down by the stairs? I would totally use the boat instead if I were you.

You just had to jump in, didn't you?

Turns out there's a ton of fish in here! One of them seems to be asking too many questions. It doesn't look too smart, but it is pretty big. It's probably a good idea to get out of there!

You've made it to safety!

Maybe next time you'll think twice about diving into scenic lakes that have freezing water and intimidating aquatic life forms! Please use a little bit more caution when you're out travelling. No point in getting out of your comfort zone if there's risk involved!
